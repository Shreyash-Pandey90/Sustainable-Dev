Donate To Save The Environment


Tree Planting

In Kurnool district, deforestation has become a major concern. SERUDS NGO is working to promote reforestation by planting trees in areas where deforestation has occurred.



Waste Management:

Improper waste management is a significant environmental problem in Kurnool district. SERUDS NGO is working to address this issue by providing waste management solutions to local communities.

Community Clean-Up:

SERUDS NGO organizes regular community clean-up events in Kurnool district. We work with local communities to identify areas that need to be cleaned up, and we provide the necessary resources and equipment to make it happen.

Water Conservation:

Kurnool district faces severe water scarcity during the summer months. SERUDS NGO is working to promote water conservation practices in the district. The role of SERUDS NGO in Water Conservation program would be to work with local communities in Kurnool district to promote rainwater harvesting and implement the techniques and practices associated with it.

SERUDS NGO can also help raise awareness about the benefits of rainwater harvesting and encourage people to adopt this practice in their communities. SERUDS NGO can also collaborate with local government bodies and other organizations to implement rainwater harvesting projects and develop infrastructure for the same.
